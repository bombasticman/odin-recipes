Kadayıf

Description

Dessert that will make you addicted

Ingredients

  • 250g fresh kadaif
  • 100 g butter, melted
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 1.5 cup sugar
  • 2 cup water
  • 1/2 slice lemon

Steps

  1. Shred half of the kadayıf into a 26 cm baking pan,
  2. Press with your palm strongly,
  3. Spread half of the butter with a spoon evenly, press again
  4. Pour the walnuts on top, press,
  5. Shred and spread the remaining kadayıf, press,
  6. Spread the remaining butter, press,
  7. Cut it in 8 triangles,
  8. Bake in a 170 C degrees pre-heated oven until golden on top (around 1 hour),
  9. Set aside to cool,
  10. For syrup combine sugar, water and slice of lemon in a sauce pan,
  11. Cook, constantly stirring until it starts to boil,
  12. Then cook for 15-20 mins. (you don't need to stir),
  13. Remove from heat and set aside for 2-3 mins.,
  14. Pour the hot syrup over cold kadayıf,
  15. You can serve after it absorbs the syrup.
  16. Afiyet Olsun.
